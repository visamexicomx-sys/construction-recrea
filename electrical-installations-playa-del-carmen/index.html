Electrical Work in the Riviera Maya: What to Know

Electrical work is one of the items where cutting corners costs the most later. In the Riviera Maya, heat and humidity demand quality materials, good grounding and surge protection. Every installation must meet the NOM-001-SEDE code and, for the contract, an electrical project approved by CFE. We size the load to your equipment (A/C, pool, kitchen), balance phases and leave the installation ready to grow (solar panels, EV charger). We do the electrical coordinated with the build and with DRO-signed drawings.
