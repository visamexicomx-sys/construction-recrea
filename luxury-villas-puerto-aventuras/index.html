Building & Renovating in Puerto Aventuras: What to Know

Puerto Aventuras is a gated community with a natural marina, golf course and controlled access, in the municipality of Solidaridad. Every project needs master-condominium administration approval: façade rules, deposit, work hours and controlled entry of materials and crew, plus the municipal license and DRO. We know the regulations and coordinate access friction-free — ideal for owners who don’t live here. Marina- and golf-front villas enjoy strong appreciation and vacation-rental demand.
