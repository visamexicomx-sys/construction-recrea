Building in Akumal: what you should know

Akumal is an ecological Caribbean-front area famous for its turtles and reefs. Building near the coast requires environmental permits, respect for the dune and the Federal Maritime Zone (ZOFEMAT), and eco-friendly water and drainage solutions. We have commercial experience (the Akumal 7-Eleven) and eco-residential expertise. High demand for beachfront vacation rentals.
