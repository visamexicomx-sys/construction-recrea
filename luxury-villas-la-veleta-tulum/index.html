Building & Renovating in La Veleta: What to Know

La Veleta is the fastest-growing residential zone of Tulum — more affordable than Aldea Zama and with the best vacation-rental ROI, in high demand from investors. A boho-chic style dominates (chukum, wood, natural tones). Being in the municipality of Tulum, every project requires SEMA environmental authorization ($50,000–$150,000 MXN, 2–4 months); jungle lots need a land-clearing permit with reforestation, and many include a cistern and water treatment. We handle SEMA and coordinate everything friction-free — ideal for investors who do not live here.
