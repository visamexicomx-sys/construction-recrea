Building & Renovating in Aldea Zama: What to Know

Aldea Zama is the premium residential zone of Tulum, a master development between the town and the hotel zone, with eco-chic design guidelines (chukum, wood, natural tones, jungle integration). Being in the municipality of Tulum, every project requires SEMA environmental authorization ($50,000–$150,000 MXN, 2–4 months); jungle lots need a land-clearing permit with reforestation, and the water grid is limited — most villas include a cistern and treatment. We know the development rules and SEMA, and coordinate everything friction-free — ideal for owners who do not live here.
