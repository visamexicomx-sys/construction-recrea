Build Your Dream Property in the Riviera Maya

Recrea Construction is a full-service construction and renovation company based in Playa del Carmen, Mexico. Since 2008, we have completed over 196 projects — from luxury beachfront villas in Tulum to commercial spaces in Cancun.

We handle everything under one roof: architectural design, municipal permits and licensing, ground-up construction, interior finishes, electrical systems, custom carpentry and metalwork. Our bilingual team works with foreign investors, expat homeowners and hospitality businesses across Quintana Roo.

Where We Build

Our crews operate across the entire Riviera Maya corridor — Cancun, Playa del Carmen, Puerto Aventuras, Akumal and Tulum. Whether you own a lot and need to build from scratch, or have an existing property that needs renovation, we bring the same quality standard to every project.

Building as a Foreigner in Mexico

Hundreds of expats and international investors trust Recrea to build their properties in Mexico. We guide you through every step — from setting up a bank trust (fideicomiso) for property ownership, to navigating local permits and managing construction while you are abroad. Weekly photo and video reports keep you informed no matter where you are.
