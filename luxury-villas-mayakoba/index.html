Building & Renovating in Mayakoba: What to Know

Mayakoba is an ultra-luxury residential and hotel development of ~240 hectares of lagoons, canals and mangrove, in the municipality of Solidaridad. It is very low density with strict design guidelines and environmental rules: the design committee approves façade, height, materials and color, and any work near the mangrove or lagoon requires environmental compliance, plus the municipal license and DRO. We know the regulations and coordinate access friction-free — ideal for owners who don’t live here. It’s the highest-appreciating address in the Riviera Maya.
