Why Custom Carpentry vs. Store-Bought

  • Exact fit — Mexican homes have non-standard dimensions. Store furniture rarely fits perfectly.
  • Climate-appropriate wood — IKEA particleboard swells and warps in tropical humidity within months.
  • Cost-effective — custom solid wood costs the same or less than imported furniture, and lasts 10× longer.
  • Airbnb aesthetic — handcrafted tropical wood is what guests expect in the Riviera Maya. It photographs beautifully.
  • Unique character — each piece is one-of-a-kind with natural wood grain patterns.
