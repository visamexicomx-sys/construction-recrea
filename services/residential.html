Our Process: 5 Steps to Your New Home

1
Free Consultation

We visit your lot (or help you find one), discuss your vision, budget, and timeline. No commitment.

2
Design & Architecture

Our architect creates floor plans, 3D renders, and structural engineering. You approve every detail. (4–8 weeks)

3
Permits & Legals

We handle all municipal permits — land use, environmental, construction license, DRO sign-off. (4–12 weeks)

4
Construction

Foundation through finishes. Weekly photo/video updates. Milestone-based payments. (8–14 months)

5
Handover

Final walkthrough, punch list, keys. 1-year structural warranty included.
