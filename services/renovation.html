Why Renovation in the Tropics Is Different

The Riviera Maya climate destroys standard materials. We use:

  • Marine-grade waterproofing — not the cheap stuff that peels in 2 years
  • Tropical hardwoods — tzalam, machiche, parota — naturally resistant to humidity and termites
  • Stainless steel hardware — regular steel rusts within months near the coast
  • Chukum plaster — the traditional Mayan waterproof coating, beautiful and functional
  • Drainage engineering — heavy tropical rains require proper slope and drainage planning
