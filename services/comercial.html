Por Qué los Negocios Eligen Recrea

  • Cumplimiento de marca — built for national franchises, follow exact brand specifications
  • Comprometidos con plazos — we plan backwards from your opening date
  • All trades in-house — electrical (3-phase), plumbing, carpentry, metalwork, signage
  • Commercial permits — Protección Civil, SEMARNAT, health department, fire safety
  • IMSS-registered crew — full liability coverage for all workers on site
