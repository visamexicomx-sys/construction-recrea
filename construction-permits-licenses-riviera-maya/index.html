What is a DRO and Why is it Mandatory?

The Responsible Works Director (DRO) is a licensed architect or engineer with municipal registration who signs the project and is legally accountable for the works before city hall: structural safety, code compliance and supervision. In Quintana Roo, no construction license is issued without a DRO. Building without one is illegal, risks fines and stop-work orders, and makes it impossible to register the addition on the deed. Recrea works with in-house architects and DROs: we include the DRO in every project and coordinate all paperwork — license, land use, SEMA, Civil Protection — so your build is 100% legal.
