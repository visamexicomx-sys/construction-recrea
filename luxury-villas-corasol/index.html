Building & Renovating in Corasol: What to Know

Corasol is a master golf and beach community north of Playa del Carmen, in the municipality of Solidaridad, with a signature golf course, private beach club and cenotes. Every project needs design-committee and administration approval: façade, height, material and work-hour rules, plus the municipal license and DRO. Our office is in Corasol, so we coordinate access, hours and approvals with full knowledge of the rules — ideal for owners who do not live here. It is one of the highest-appreciating areas in the Riviera Maya.
