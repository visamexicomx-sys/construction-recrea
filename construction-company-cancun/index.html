Building in Cancún: what you should know

Cancún combines the most active commercial market in Quintana Roo with fast-growing residential areas. In the Hotel Zone, condo renovation and tourist retail dominate (FONATUR rules and restricted site access); in Puerto Cancún and Marina Town Center, premium retail; and in the Supermanzanas and Puerto Morelos, homes and residential developments at a better cost than Playa del Carmen or Tulum.

For commercial work we handle the operating license, Civil Protection approval, electrical project with CFE and internal safety program — we deliver units ready to operate.
