How Much Does It Cost to Build a House in Playa del Carmen?

In 2026, building a house in Playa del Carmen costs between $12,000 and $25,000 MXN per m² depending on finishes — a 150 m² home with a pool runs about $2.7–$4.5 million MXN. Here's the full breakdown and what drives the price.

Quick answer: $12,000–$16,000 MXN/m² (economy), $16,000–$25,000 (mid), $25,000–$40,000 (luxury). A 150 m² turnkey home: ~$2.7–$4.5M MXN. Estimate your budget →

What Drives the Cost of Your House

Land & location

Lot price, soil type, utility access and zone (Playacar and beachfront cost more).

Size & levels

More m² and a second story raise the total, though they lower the cost per m².

Finish level

Economy, mid or luxury (chukum, stone, home automation) is the biggest price driver.

Pool & extras

Infinity pool, cistern, solar panels, walls and landscaping are quoted separately.

Permits & DRO

Land use, license, environmental studies and Responsible Works Director.

Legal labor

IMSS-registered workers and a written warranty cost a bit more but protect you.

How Long Does It Take & What's Included?

  1. Design and permits: 2–4 months (project, DRO, licenses).
  2. Foundation and structure: 2–4 months.
  3. Systems and finishes: 4–8 months.
  4. Total turnkey: 8–14 months of construction by size.
  5. Includes: architecture, permits, build, electrical, plumbing, carpentry, ironwork and final cleaning.
