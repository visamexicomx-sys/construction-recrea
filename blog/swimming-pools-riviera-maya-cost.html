Swimming Pools in the Riviera Maya: Types, Cost & Maintenance

June 2026 • Recrea Construction • 5 min read

Key takeaway: a pool is the #1 investment in the Riviera Maya. It adds $20,000–$30,000 USD to property value and boosts Airbnb nightly rates by $30–$60.

Pool Types

Concrete Pool (Most Popular)

The standard for the Riviera Maya. Built on-site from reinforced concrete, finished with mosaic, chukum plaster, or tile. Full freedom of shape and size.

  • Standard (4×8 m): $14,000–$22,000 USD
  • Large (5×10 m): $22,000–$33,000 USD
  • Plunge pool (2×4 m): $6,500–$11,000 USD

Infinity Edge Pool

Visually stunning, especially on sloped lots or with a view. 30–50% more expensive than standard due to overflow engineering.

  • Cost: $22,000–$45,000 USD

Rooftop Pool

Popular for condos and boutique hotels. Requires reinforced roof structure and waterproofing. Most expensive option.

  • Cost: $28,000–$67,000 USD

Our pick: chukum — a traditional Mayan plaster, perfect for the tropics. Naturally waterproof, beautiful turquoise tone, photographs beautifully for Airbnb listings.

Monthly Maintenance

  • Chemicals (chlorine, pH, algaecide): $45–$85 USD/month
  • Electricity (pump 6–8 hrs/day): $55–$140 USD/month
  • Pool service (professional): $110–$220 USD/month (2–3 visits/week)
  • Total: $210–$440 USD/month

If you're renting on Airbnb, maintenance pays for itself in 1–2 extra nights of income from having a pool.

Building Pools in Quintana Roo: What's Different

  • Limestone bedrock: The Yucatán Peninsula is solid limestone. Excavation requires jackhammers or mini-excavators. Budget +10–15% for groundwork.
  • High water table: Coastal areas can have water at 1–2 m depth. Enhanced waterproofing and drainage systems are essential.
  • Salt air: Equipment must be stainless steel or plastic. Regular metal corrodes within a season.
  • Hurricanes: The pool itself won't be damaged, but the deck and equipment can. Plan a protective enclosure for pump and filter.

Build Timeline

  • Plunge pool: 3–4 weeks
  • Standard 4×8 m: 4–6 weeks
  • Rooftop pool: 6–10 weeks
  • Infinity edge: 6–8 weeks
