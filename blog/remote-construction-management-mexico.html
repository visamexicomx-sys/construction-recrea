Remote Construction Management: Build Your Mexico Home from Abroad

80% of our clients live in the US, Canada, Europe, or Asia while their home is being built in Mexico. Remote construction management isn't a compromise — at Recrea, it's our standard operating model.

How Remote Construction Works

Daily Photo Reports

Every day by 6 PM Mexico time, your project manager sends 10–15 photos via WhatsApp showing the day's progress. You see every wall, every pipe, every detail as it happens.

Weekly Video Walkthroughs

Every Friday, a 20–30 minute live video call where your PM walks through the entire site, camera in hand. Ask questions, request close-ups, discuss next week's plan.

Real-Time Project Dashboard

Access your project's Gantt chart, budget tracker, and photo gallery through our cloud platform. See exactly where you stand on timeline and spending — anytime, from anywhere.

Monthly Drone Footage

Aerial video and photos showing overall progress, roof work, and site context. Shared via private YouTube link.

Decision-Making Without Being On-Site

Material Selection

We send product photos, samples via mail, and video comparisons. For major decisions (floor tile, kitchen counters, exterior finish), we create a digital mood board with 3 options and pricing.

Design Changes

Need to modify the plan? Our architects update 3D renders within 48 hours. You approve changes via email or WhatsApp before any work proceeds.

Quality Checkpoints

At 6 critical milestones (foundation, structure, roof, rough-in, finishes, final), we conduct a formal video inspection. Nothing proceeds until you approve.
