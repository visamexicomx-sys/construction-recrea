Outdoor Kitchens & Palapas in the Riviera Maya 2026

Year-round tropical weather makes outdoor living spaces essential. A well-designed outdoor kitchen boosts property value and Airbnb revenue significantly.

Types of outdoor spaces

Traditional palapa

  • Cost: $3,500–$8,000 USD
  • Wood frame with palm roof, 20–40 m²

Full outdoor kitchen

  • Cost: $8,000–$25,000 USD
  • Built-in grill, bar, sink, refrigerator

Premium entertainment area

  • Cost: $25,000–$55,000 USD
  • Kitchen + dining + lounge + wood-fired oven + lighting

Recommended materials

Airbnb ROI

Properties with outdoor kitchens and palapas report 20–35% higher nightly rates vs. properties without outdoor areas.
