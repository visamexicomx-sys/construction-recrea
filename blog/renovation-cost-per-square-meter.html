Renovation Cost Per Square Meter in Playa del Carmen 2026

Updated June 2026 • By Recrea Construction • 8 min read

Quick answer: Renovation in Playa del Carmen costs $4,000–$18,000 MXN/m² ($220–$1,000 USD) depending on scope and finish level.

Renovation Costs by Type

Renovation TypeMXN/m²USD/m²
Cosmetic — paint, fixtures, hardware$4,000–$6,000$220–$330
Moderate — new floors, kitchen/bath, lighting$6,000–$10,000$330–$555
Full gut — structural, layout changes, new MEP$10,000–$15,000$555–$830
Luxury/high-end — imported finishes, smart home$15,000–$18,000+$830–$1,000+

What Affects Renovation Cost

  • Scope of work — cosmetic refresh vs. structural modification. Moving walls, plumbing, or electrical adds 30-50% to the base cost.
  • Material quality — local porcelain tile ($200 MXN/m²) vs. imported Italian marble ($2,500 MXN/m²). Materials typically represent 40-50% of total renovation cost.
  • Property condition — renovating a 5-year-old condo vs. a 30-year-old house. Older properties often reveal hidden issues (termite damage, corroded rebar, outdated wiring) that increase costs.
  • Access and location — ground floor units are cheaper to renovate than 5th-floor condos where materials must be carried up stairs. Gated communities may restrict work hours and equipment access.
  • Season — May–September (low season) is 10-15% cheaper for labor and some materials.

Renovation vs. New Construction

When renovation costs approach 60-70% of new construction cost per m², building new often makes more sense. The threshold is roughly:

  • Renovate when the structure is sound, layout works, and you're mainly updating finishes and systems
  • Build new when you need layout changes, the structure has significant damage, or the building doesn't meet current codes
