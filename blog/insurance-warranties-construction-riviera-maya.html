Warranties Your Builder Should Offer

  • Structural: minimum 5 years — foundation, columns, beams, slabs
  • Waterproofing: 3–5 years depending on system
  • Finishes: 1 year — paint, flooring, carpentry
  • Systems: 2 years — electrical, plumbing, gas
  • Equipment: manufacturer warranty — AC, heaters, pumps

Performance Bonds

  • Advance payment bond: protects your down payment (10–30% of contract)
  • Performance bond: 10% of total value, guarantees completion
  • Hidden defects bond: 10% for 12 months post-delivery
  • Typical cost: 2–5% of bonded amount
