Bathroom Renovation Cost in Playa del Carmen (2026)

Bathroom renovations deliver the second-highest ROI after kitchens — especially for Airbnb properties where guest reviews focus heavily on bathroom quality. Here's what it costs in Playa del Carmen.

Timeline

Budget: 1–2 weeks. Mid-range: 2–3 weeks. Premium/spa-style: 3–5 weeks.

Tropical Bathroom Essentials

  • Waterproofing: Critical in Quintana Roo's humidity. We use 3-layer membrane systems on all wet walls and floors.
  • Ventilation: Exhaust fan or louvered windows mandatory. Mold prevention is #1 priority.
  • Rain showerheads: Standard in mid-range+ renovations. $80–$300 installed.
  • Chukum finish: Tulum-style waterproof plaster for shower walls. $35–$60/m². Stunning and functional.
  • LED mirror: $150–$500. Instantly elevates the space.

What Airbnb Guests Care About

  1. Hot water pressure — Install a proper tankless heater ($400–$800)
  2. Clean modern tiles — Large format tiles (60×60 or larger) look cleaner
  3. Glass shower enclosure — No shower curtains in premium listings
  4. Good lighting — LED vanity mirror + ceiling light
  5. Quality towel bars and hooks — Stainless steel, not plastic
