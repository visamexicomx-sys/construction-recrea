Air Conditioning & Ventilation in the Riviera Maya 2026

With year-round temperatures of 28-35°C and 70-90% humidity, AC is essential in the Riviera Maya. A well-planned installation during construction saves up to 40% on energy costs vs. a retrofit.

System Types

Mini Split (Most Common)

  • Cost installed: $450-$1,200 USD per unit
  • Advantage: simple install, zone control, affordable

VRF / Multi-Split

  • Cost complete: $4,500-$11,000 USD
  • Best for: homes 200+ m², luxury apartments

Ducted (Central)

  • Cost: $6,500-$16,500 USD
  • Advantage: invisible, uniform distribution
  • Must plan during design phase

Inverter vs Conventional

  • Energy savings: 30-50% vs conventional
  • Lifespan: 15-20 years vs 8-12
  • Extra cost: 40-60% more upfront
  • ROI: pays for itself in 2-3 years with daily use

BTU Sizing (Tropical Rule)

  • 800-1,000 BTU per m² (vs 600 in temperate climates)
  • High ceilings (+3m): add 20%
  • Large windows: add 15-25%
  • Kitchen: add 4,000 BTU
  • Top floor: add 20% for roof solar radiation
