Land Prices in Playa del Carmen & Tulum (2026)

Before you build, you buy land — and prices vary wildly by zone. In 2026, expect $1,500 MXN/m² in emerging areas up to $30,000+/m² in Playacar. Here is the real per-m² map for Playa del Carmen and Tulum.

What Moves the Price

Location and services drive everything: paved roads, CFE electricity, water and legal title (escritura, not just ejido rights) command a premium. Beachfront and gated communities cost the most. Always verify the land is regularized and buildable before purchase.

Land + Build: the Real Budget

A realistic rule of thumb: land is 25–45% of a finished home's cost in the Riviera Maya. On a mid-range 150 m² home costing ~$2.7–4.5M MXN to build, budget the lot on top. Compare zones on our interactive price map and estimate construction on the cost-to-build guide.

Buyer Tips Before You Build

Confirm clean title and use-of-sufrom (uso de suelo) that permits your project. Check flood risk, cenote proximity and subsoil (karst) — they affect foundation cost. Budget for permits and, in Tulum, the SEMA environmental authorization. As a construction company in Playa del Carmen, we review lots with clients before they buy.
