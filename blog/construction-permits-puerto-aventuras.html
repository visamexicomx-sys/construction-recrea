Construction Permits in Puerto Aventuras (2026)

Puerto Aventuras is a gated marina community in the municipality of Solidaridad. You need the standard municipal permits — plus approval from the community administration. Here is the full process, costs and timeline in 2026.

Permits & Approvals You Need

  • Uso de suelo and municipal licencia de construcción (Solidaridad / Playa del Carmen City Hall)
  • DRO — a licensed Responsible Works Director
  • Community / HOA approval — the master condominium administration must authorize the project
  • Work-hour & access coordination — material and crew entry follow community rules
  • CFE / water feasibility for connections
  • Environmental permits if the lot is near mangrove or the marina

The Gated-Community Factor

Unlike an open lot, building in Puerto Aventuras requires coordination with the administration of the master condominium: design/aesthetic approval, deposit, work hours, and controlled access for materials and workers. Getting this right avoids fines and stop-work orders — and is exactly why owners hire a builder who knows the community rules.
