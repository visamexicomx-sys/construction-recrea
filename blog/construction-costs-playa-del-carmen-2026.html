How to Save 15–20% on Construction

  1. Build during low season (May–September) — labor and materials are 10–15% cheaper
  2. Use local materials — chukum plaster, local stone, tropical hardwoods instead of imports
  3. One contractor for everything — avoid 5 separate subcontractors with markup on each
  4. Simple geometry — rectangular floor plans cost 20% less than L-shapes and curves
  5. Skip the basement — high water table in Riviera Maya makes basements extremely expensive

5 Mistakes That Add $20,000+ to Your Build

  1. No soil study — hitting rock or cenote during foundation = $5,000–$15,000 extra
  2. Hiring by lowest price — cheap contractors cut corners on rebar, waterproofing, electrical
  3. No written contract — scope creep without a fixed-price contract adds 15–25%
  4. Ignoring hurricane codes — retrofitting later costs 3× more than building right
  5. Changing design mid-build — moving a wall after concrete is poured = demolish + rebuild
