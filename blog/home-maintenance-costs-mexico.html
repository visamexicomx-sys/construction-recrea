Home Maintenance Costs in Mexico: Full Breakdown

June 2026 • Recrea Construction • 5 min read

Bottom line: maintaining a 150 m² home with pool in Playa del Carmen costs $440–$830 USD/month. That's 3–5× cheaper than a comparable home in Miami or Los Angeles.

How to Reduce Costs

  1. Solar panels — cut electricity bill by 60–80%. Payback in 4–5 years.
  2. Inverter A/C units — use half the energy of standard units.
  3. LED lighting — saves $28–$55 USD/month.
  4. Rainwater collection — reduces water usage by 30–50%.
  5. Roof insulation — reduces heat and A/C demand.
