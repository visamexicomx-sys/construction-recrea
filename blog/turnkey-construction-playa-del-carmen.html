Turnkey Construction in Playa del Carmen & Tulum

Turnkey construction means one company handles everything — from architectural design to handing you the keys. No juggling architects, engineers, contractors, and interior designers separately. At Recrea, turnkey is our specialty.

What's Included in Turnkey Construction?

  • Architecture & Design: Floor plans, 3D renderings, structural engineering, MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing) design
  • Permits: Construction license, land use certificate, environmental studies, municipal approvals
  • Construction: Foundation, structure, roofing, waterproofing, all trades
  • Installations: Electrical, plumbing, A/C, water cistern, pump, solar pre-wiring
  • Finishes: Floors, walls, ceilings, kitchen, bathrooms, closets, painting
  • Exterior: Landscaping, driveway, perimeter walls, pool (optional)
  • Furnishing: Optional — we source locally and ship from Mérida/Mexico City
  • Connections: CFE (electricity), CAPA (water), internet

Why Turnkey Beats Managing Separately

1. Single Point of Accountability

When the architect blames the contractor and the contractor blames the electrician, nobody fixes the problem. With turnkey, Recrea owns every outcome.

2. 15–25% Cost Savings

We buy materials at contractor prices, eliminate coordination waste between separate firms, and prevent the costly rework that happens when trades don't communicate.

3. Faster Timeline

No gaps between design handoff and construction start. Our teams overlap — interior design begins while structure is still going up.

4. Perfect for Remote Owners

Most of our clients live abroad. Turnkey means you get daily photo updates, weekly video calls, and a dedicated project manager — without ever needing to be on-site.
