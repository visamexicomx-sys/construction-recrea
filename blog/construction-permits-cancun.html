Construction Permits in Cancún (2026)

Cancún (municipality of Benito Juárez) is Quintana Roo's busiest commercial market, with specific rules in the Hotel Zone. Here is what permits you need for homes and commercial projects, the costs and the timeline in 2026.

Permits You Need in Cancún

  • Uso de suelo — land-use certificate for your lot and project type
  • Licencia de construcción — building license from Benito Juárez City Hall
  • DRO — licensed Responsible Works Director signing the project
  • Civil Protection approval — required, especially for commercial units
  • CFE electrical project — for the power connection
  • Licencia de funcionamiento — operating license for commercial/restaurants

Hotel Zone & Beachfront Rules

Projects in the Hotel Zone follow FONATUR guidelines and have restricted site access and work hours. Beachfront work touches the Federal Maritime Zone (ZOFEMAT) and needs additional authorization. Condo renovations must be coordinated with the building administration.

Residential vs. Commercial

A home in the Supermanzanas or Puerto Morelos permits faster than a Hotel Zone commercial unit. Commercial and franchise builds add the operating license, Civil Protection sign-off and CFE project — we deliver units ready to operate.
