Commercial Construction in Cancún and the Riviera Maya

The Riviera Maya's booming tourism and real estate markets are driving demand for commercial spaces — restaurants, retail shops, convenience stores, offices, clinics, and boutique hotels. Building a commercial property here requires a contractor who understands both the construction and the regulatory landscape.

Types of Commercial Projects We Build

Retail and Convenience Stores

We've built multiple convenience store locations including 7-Eleven and GoMart across the Riviera Maya. These projects require fast turnarounds, strict brand specifications, and coordination with franchise operations teams. Typical build time: 3–5 months.

Restaurants and Bars

Restaurant construction in the Riviera Maya involves specialized requirements — commercial kitchen ventilation, grease traps, health department compliance, sound insulation, and outdoor terrace construction. We handle design through final health inspection.

Offices and Coworking Spaces

With the remote work boom, Playa del Carmen and Tulum are seeing strong demand for modern office spaces. We build out everything from single professional offices to multi-floor coworking facilities.

Boutique Hotels and Hostels

Small hospitality projects (5–30 rooms) are one of the most popular commercial investments in Tulum and Playa. These require specialized permits, fire safety systems, and accessibility compliance.

Medical Clinics and Dental Offices

Cancún and Playa del Carmen are major medical tourism destinations. Clinic construction requires specific ventilation, electrical, and sanitation standards.

Commercial Construction Costs

  • Basic commercial shell (warehouse, simple retail): $8,000–$12,000 MXN/m²
  • Standard retail/office (finished, A/C ready): $12,000–$18,000 MXN/m²
  • Restaurant/bar (full kitchen, bar, outdoor area): $15,000–$25,000 MXN/m²
  • Boutique hotel (fully finished rooms, common areas): $18,000–$30,000 MXN/m²

Commercial Permits

Commercial construction requires additional permits beyond residential:

  • Commercial land use license (uso de suelo comercial)
  • Environmental impact assessment
  • Construction license
  • Protección Civil (civil protection/fire safety) sign-off
  • Health department permits (for food service)
  • Alcohol license (for bars/restaurants — separate process through the state)
  • Accessibility compliance verification

We manage all commercial permitting in-house. Our team has worked with municipal offices across Solidaridad, Benito Juárez (Cancún), and Tulum municipalities for 18+ years.
