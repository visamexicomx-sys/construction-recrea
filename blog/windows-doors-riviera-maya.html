Frequently Asked Questions

Hurricane-rated aluminum windows with impact glass (laminated). ASTM E1886/E1996 certification. Minimum: large-missile impact test. PVC/vinyl windows: cheaper but warp in tropical heat and UV. Wood windows: beautiful but need constant maintenance. Aluminum is the clear winner.

Standard aluminum + impact glass: $3,500–$5,500 MXN/m². Premium (thermal break, better frames): $5,500–$7,000/m². Sliding glass doors (large): $6,000–$10,000/m². A 150m² home with 20m² of windows: $70,000–$140,000 MXN. Investing in quality windows saves on A/C, insurance, and hurricane damage.

Entry doors: solid wood or aluminum ($8,000–$30,000). Interior doors: solid-core wood ($3,000–$8,000 each). Sliding glass patio doors: $15,000–$40,000 per panel. Folding glass walls (4–6m opening): $80,000–$200,000 — the signature feature of tropical indoor-outdoor living.

Impact windows alone meet building code for hurricane protection. Storm shutters are a second layer — recommended for beachfront or high-exposure locations. Accordion shutters: $2,000–$5,000 per opening. Roll-down: $4,000–$10,000. Colonial shutters: $3,000–$6,000 (decorative + functional).

Impact glass reduces UV by 99% (protecting interiors) and heat transmission by 25–40%. Double-pane (insulated) options reduce A/C costs by 30–40%. Over 10 years, energy savings can equal 20–30% of the window investment. Plus: 15–25% insurance premium reduction.

Our 6-Step Process

01
Opening Schedule

Define every window and door: location, size, type, operation (fixed, sliding, casement). Optimize for ventilation and views.

02
Specification

Select frame type, glass type (impact laminated, insulated, tinted), hardware, screen options. Match to wind zone requirements.

03
Manufacturing

Custom sizes fabricated by certified manufacturer. 4–8 weeks production. Quality inspection before delivery.

04
Rough Openings

During construction: precise openings formed in walls. Sill pan flashing and waterproof membrane installed.

05
Installation

Professional installation with proper shimming, leveling, sealing. Spray-foam insulation around frames.

06
Testing

Water test every window (hose method). Verify hardware operation. Confirm proper drainage. Warranty registration.
