These pay for themselves in energy savings and damage prevention.

Basic (smart locks + lights + thermostat): $15,000–$30,000 MXN. Mid-range (add cameras, leak sensors, blinds): $30,000–$80,000. Full automation (integrated hub, scene control, energy monitoring): $80,000–$200,000. Pre-wiring during construction saves 40–60% vs retrofit.

WiFi-based systems work best (reliable, no proprietary hub needed). Recommended: Shelly (switches/relays, local control), Tuya/Smart Life (budget devices), Apple HomeKit, Google Home. Z-Wave and Zigbee also work but need hub. Avoid: systems requiring US-only servers or subscriptions.

Telmex fiber: 100–500 Mbps, $500–$900 MXN/month (best reliability). Totalplay: similar speeds. 4G/5G backup: $300–$500/month. For smart home: minimum 50 Mbps. Always install backup internet — if WiFi goes down, your smart locks still work via Bluetooth but nothing else responds.
