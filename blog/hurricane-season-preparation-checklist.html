Hurricane Preparation Checklist

Before the Season (May)
  • Inspect roof membrane — repair any damage
  • Clear all roof drains and gutters
  • Test all storm shutters/impact windows
  • Service generator and stock fuel
  • Trim trees within 3m of building
  • Review and update insurance
  • Document all property contents (video)
When a Storm Threatens
  • Close all shutters and secure doors
  • Bring ALL outdoor furniture inside
  • Fill cistern and bathtubs with water
  • Stock 5 days of water and food
  • Charge all devices and power banks
  • Withdraw cash (ATMs may go offline)
  • Know your evacuation route

If You're Not in Mexico

  • Property management company — pay for hurricane prep service ($110–$275 USD)
  • Trusted neighbor — arrange in advance who closes shutters and secures furniture
  • Security cameras — for remote monitoring (while power lasts)
  • Motorized shutters — roll-down shutters with electric motor can be closed remotely
