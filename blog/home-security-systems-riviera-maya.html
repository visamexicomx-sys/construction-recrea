Home Security Systems in the Riviera Maya 2026

Property security in the Riviera Maya has two dimensions: intrusion protection and hurricane protection. A well-designed system installed during construction costs up to 50% less than retrofitting.

CCTV Cameras

  • Basic 4-camera IP system: $450-$830 USD
  • 8-camera with NVR, 30-day storage: $830-$1,650 USD
  • Recommended brands: Hikvision, Dahua, Reolink
  • Tip: POE wiring during construction is 10x cheaper than wireless after

Access Control

  • Smart lock (Yale, August): $220-$440 USD per door
  • Video doorbell (Ring, Hikvision): $165-$440 USD
  • Automatic gate with remote: $830-$1,940 USD
  • Airbnb advantage: temporary codes per guest, no physical keys

Alarm & Monitoring

  • Wireless alarm kit (Ajax, DSC): $440-$1,100 USD
  • 24/7 monitoring with armed response: $45-$110 USD/month

Hurricane Protection

  • Roll-down aluminum shutters: $140-$275 USD/m²
  • Removable steel panels: $45-$85 USD/m²
  • Miami-Dade laminated glass: $195-$390 USD/m² (permanent)
