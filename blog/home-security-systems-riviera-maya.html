Our 6-Step Process

01
Security Assessment

Evaluate your property's vulnerabilities: access points, lighting, sightlines, neighbor proximity, occupancy pattern.

02
System Design

Camera placement, sensor locations, smart lock selection, lighting plan. Integrate with home automation.
