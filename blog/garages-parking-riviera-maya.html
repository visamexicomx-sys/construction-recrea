Garages & Parking in the Riviera Maya 2026

Parking is a municipal requirement for all new construction. A covered garage costs $650–$1,200 USD/m² and protects your vehicle from sun, rain, and hurricanes.

Municipal Requirements

  • Residential: minimum 1 space per dwelling (Solidaridad)
  • Commercial: 1 space per 30–40 m² of construction
  • Minimum dimensions: 2.50 × 5.00 m per space + circulation
  • Accessibility: handicapped spaces required in commercial

Recommended Materials

  • Floor: stamped or polished concrete ($250–$450 MXN/m²)
  • Structure: reinforced concrete or galvanized steel
  • Gate: aluminum ($8,000–$15,000 MXN) or steel ($5,000–$12,000 MXN)
  • Roof: concrete slab, metal frame with sheet, or polycarbonate
