Building & Renovating in the Hotel Zone: What to Know

The Cancun Hotel Zone is the 22 km beachfront strip along Boulevard Kukulcan, in the municipality of Benito Juarez, master-planned by FONATUR. It is the highest-appreciating area of Cancun, dominated by beachfront residences, penthouses and condos. Every project follows the FONATUR guidelines (tourist land use, density, image) with restricted access and work hours; beachfront work requires ZOFEMAT authorization (Federal Maritime Zone), plus the Benito Juarez license and DRO. Salt air calls for anti-corrosion ironwork and marine finishes. We know the regulations and coordinate access and hours friction-free — ideal for owners who do not live here.
