Kitchen Styles & Trends in the Riviera Maya

Three styles dominate in Playa del Carmen: modern minimalist (clean lines, handleless cabinets, quartz counters), contemporary tropical (wood, chukum, natural tones) and the open island kitchen integrated with the living area, ideal for the warm climate and rental properties. The humid climate calls for anti-corrosion hardware, good ventilation and durable materials — we factor this into every design.
