We build pools across the Riviera Maya

Playa del Carmen

Our home base. Residential pools in Playacar, Centro, Ejidal and Corasol. Municipal permits in 2–4 weeks — the fastest in the region. Specialty: pools with integrated decks and rooftop plunge pools.

Tulum

The eco-chic pool capital: chukum finish, organic shapes, beach entries. We handle the mandatory SEMA permit with a 100% approval rate. Limestone terrain: specialized excavation included in the price.

Cancún

Residential pools in the Supermanzanas and commercial pools for hotels and condos in the Hotel Zone — FONATUR compliance, work coordinated with HOAs. Experience with large-format pools and commercial equipment.

Puerto Aventuras

Marina-front and canal-side pools: marine-grade materials (316 stainless, naval aluminum), reinforced corrosion protection. Pool renovations in gated communities since 2010.

Akumal y Puerto Morelos

Protected marine area: we design filtration systems with zero aquifer discharge and handle the special environmental permits. Low-footprint pools for eco-residences.

Bacalar

The Lagoon of Seven Colors demands maximum environmental care: freshwater pools with natural treatment, bio-pools and low-chemical systems for eco-hotels.
